About Us

Funk in the Trunk is a band born and raised in Vancouver, BC. We enjoy expressing ourselves through the sounds and beats of our instruments. Music is our passion, and we focus on making a big sound in an intimate setting.



Parker

Parker started playing the guitar when he was 13 after his friend suggested it to him, and has been in love with it ever since.

Nicky

Nicky has been playing trombone for 10 years. He first picked up the instrument as a 10 year old because he thought it sounded funny, but fell in love. He loves playing funk music, and thinks the trombone is perfect for that sound.

Dylan

Dylan started playing the saxophone when he was 10 years old. He loves the versatility of the instrument and being able to grab the attention of the crowd.

Juan

Juan has been playing drums since he was 13. He started off with latin drums, and later began exploring different types of jazz percussion. His biggest influence is Tony Williams.

Chad

Chad originally played the trumpet, but started playing bass guitar when he was 13. He loves the sound and groove. His favourite type of music is smooth jazz.
